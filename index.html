Review and post videos to TikTok

AutoPost-Studio is a self-hosted web app that lets anyone publish pre-made videos to their own TikTok account. Run it on your own machine — no shared server, no third-party storing your credentials.

1
Register your own TikTok app
Create an app at developers.tiktok.com with Login Kit and Content Posting API enabled. You get your own credentials.
2
Run it on your computer
Clone the repo, set your credentials as environment variables, and run python app.py. Opens at localhost:5000.
3
Connect your TikTok account
Click "Connect TikTok Account" and log in through TikTok's official OAuth flow. Your token is stored only on your own device.
4
Review and publish
Drop a video in the review folder. The app shows a preview, lets you set privacy and interaction settings, and requires explicit consent before posting.

What the app does

Uses TikTok Login Kit (OAuth 2.0) to connect your account — no passwords stored
Shows your TikTok identity before posting so you always know which account is connected
Requires you to manually select a privacy level — nothing is pre-selected
Comment, duet, and stitch are off by default — disabled entirely if your account settings turn them off
Requires an explicit consent checkbox before the Publish button activates
All credentials stay on your own device — no external server involved
