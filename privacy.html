Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

AutoPost-Studio is a self-hosted, open source tool. Each person who uses it runs their own copy on their own device, with their own TikTok app credentials. There is no central server, no shared account, and no data collected by this project.

1. How the app works

AutoPost-Studio is downloaded and run locally by each user on their own computer. It is not a hosted service. When you run AutoPost-Studio:

  • The app runs entirely on your own device.
  • You supply your own TikTok app credentials (client key and client secret) from the TikTok Developer Portal.
  • You authorize your own TikTok account through TikTok's official OAuth 2.0 login flow.
  • Your access token is stored only on your own device, in a local file.
  • No data is sent to or stored on any server operated by this project.

2. Information collected by this website

This website (autopost-studio.github.io) is a static informational page hosted on GitHub Pages. It does not collect, store, or process any personal data. No cookies, analytics, or tracking scripts are used.

3. TikTok account access

When you run AutoPost-Studio on your own device, it uses TikTok Login Kit to authenticate your TikTok account via OAuth 2.0. The following scopes are used:

  • user.info.basic — used only to confirm the identity of the account you have authorized, so the app can display your TikTok username and avatar on the review screen.
  • video.publish — used to publish videos directly to your TikTok profile via TikTok's Content Posting API (Direct Post). The app requires you to manually select a privacy level and confirm consent before any video is published.

The app only accesses the TikTok account you personally authorize. It does not access any other TikTok user's account, data, followers, messages, or content.

4. Video content

Videos are stored locally on your own device. They are sent directly from your device to TikTok's API when you publish. No video content passes through or is stored on any server operated by this project.

5. Credentials and tokens

Your TikTok API credentials and access tokens are stored only on your own device as local files. They are never transmitted to this project or any third party outside of TikTok's own API. If you believe your credentials have been compromised, revoke access immediately through TikTok's developer or account security settings.

6. TikTok's own privacy practices

When you use TikTok through this app, TikTok's own Privacy Policy and Terms of Service also apply. This project is not affiliated with or endorsed by TikTok.

7. Children's privacy

This app is not directed at children under the age of 13. It does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

8. Changes to this policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
